Sap hopper / Leaf hopper

Symptoms of Damage

1. Leaves become yellow
2. Covered with black sooty mould
3. Top leaves get dried up and lateral buds germinate

Identification of Pest

1. Nymph - Soft, pale brown dorsally and pale orange ventrally
2. Adult - Straw coloured, head pointing forward as a snou

Remedy

1. Avoid excessive use of nitrogenous fertilizers
2. Set up light trap
3. Detrash: 150 and 210th DAP
4. Release lepidopteran parasitoid:
Epiricrania melanoleuca @8000 -10,000 cocoon /ha (or) 8 - 10 lacs egg/ha.
5. Spray any one of the following on the 150th and 210th day (1000 l spray fluid)
Malathion 50 EC 2000 ml
Monocrotophos 36 WSC 2000 ml
