Panama Wilt

Symptoms

1. yellowing of lower leaves including leaf blades
2. leaves hang around pseudostem and wither

Cause

Warm soil temperature ,poor drainage ,light soils and high soil moisture

Remedy

1. Severly affected plant should be uprooted and burnt
2. Grow paddy followed by banana for 3-5 years once or twice
3. Use of quick lime near the base of plant
